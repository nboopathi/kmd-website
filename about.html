About Us

We build for a Green Future...

Welcome to KMD Solar Energy +, the leading division of KMD Developers dedicated to the solar power field. Since our establishment in 2017, we have been committed to building a green future by providing reliable and quality solar power solutions. With our expert knowledge, skilled workforce, and a focus on customer satisfaction, we have successfully served various industry power requirements through ground and rooftop projects. Our services encompass everything from pile foundations and solar module erection to electrical DC and AC activities, rooftop installations, and commissioning.

Our Expertise and Commitment

At KMD Solar Energy +, we take pride in our proficient and experienced team of professionals who are dedicated to the design and construction of each individual solar project. With a deep understanding of the industry and a commitment to excellence, we ensure that our projects are completed within budget and time schedule, exceeding our clients' expectations.
